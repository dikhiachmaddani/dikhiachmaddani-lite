**WRI and HMTI E-Voting System** is a comprehensive electronic voting platform designed specifically for **JTI (Jurusan Teknologi Informasi)** students to participate in organizational decision-making processes. This secure, confidential, and user-friendly platform modernizes traditional voting methods by providing a digital solution that ensures transparency, security, and accessibility for all student voters.

## Project Overview

The E-Voting System addresses the need for efficient, secure, and accessible voting mechanisms within academic organizations. By digitizing the voting process and provides real-time results while maintaining the highest standards of data security and voter privacy.
